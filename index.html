Project 2

Ever seen wings that can bend like a bird's on an airplane? Maybe, but if you're like most of us who hven't, you sure will soon enough! As of late, a new concept of Flex wings has been on the news lately, and it means we can finally do something about the fact that airplanes emmitt so much Carbon Dioxide! Basically, i'm trying to make and prove the validity of this new concept of wings by building a replica of an airplane (Airbus A320) using, wait for it, PLA Plastic (That gooey white stuff that comes out of 3D printer nozzle and is somehow really strong), but only the frame, as it is too strong to make up the wing. That, will be made with "Thermosetting polyimide", basically another gooey white stubstance that's even stronger and more flexible. I have the base done, and I need to order the Thermosetting Polyimide off amazon, which i'm going to do this week.
